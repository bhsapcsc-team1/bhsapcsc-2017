askBYS is a service of Bainbridge Youth Services (BYS), a 50 year old organization whose mission is to promote the social and emotional well being of teens and their families.

With the askBYS app you can:

  • Request an in-person appointment with a BYS therapist (we are located in Bainbridge Island, WA).
  • Ask questions anonymously to BYS therapists and medical doctors.
  • Read selected questions raised by other teens and see responses given by BYS.
  • Connect with us on social media via Facebook, Twitter, and Google Calendar.

Visit us at askBYS.org for more resources and information about our organization.
